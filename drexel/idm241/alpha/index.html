IDM 241 - Alpha: Netflix NavBar Micro Interaction

https://www.netflix.com/browse

- Current state of the NavBar Micro Interaction -

Triggers

On-Hover, Off-Hover.

Rules

On-Hover: Ease, darken font.

Off-Hover: Ease, lighten back to default.

Feedback

Visual: On-Hover, the darkened font indicates/validates pointer focus.

Loops & Modes

None.

- Concept for redesigned Micro Interaction -

Triggers

On-Hover, Off-Hover

Rules

On-Hover:

- First create a dark gray, rounded ellipse behind the icon.

- Next expand the ellipse to the right while simultaneously fading in text respective of the icon's page.

- Finally draw a white underline starting from left, expanding to the right.

Off-Hover: Rewind the effects of on-hover.

Feedback

Visual: On-Hover, the darkened font indicates/validates pointer focus.

Loops & Modes

None.
